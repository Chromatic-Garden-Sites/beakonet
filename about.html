Beako networks is run by one person, providing services mainly for themself and a few other select people. It however is also running some public stuff for other people to use. This site mainly serves as a directory to find things running on Beako networks.

Beako networks operates ASNs 54681, 60379, and 56808. We also provide transit services in the US east region via tunnel, and VMs.

I maintain an selective peering policy via tunnel/IX(soon). Peer with me by dropping an email to bgp@beako.net

The main requirement to peer with me is we should be doing some kind of traffic, there's no point to peering with no traffic between us.

Network issues and abuse complaints should be directed to noc@beako.net

Copyright complaints should be directed to unmonitored@beako.net
